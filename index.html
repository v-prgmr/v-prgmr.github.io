Who I Am

I love building robots. I started out as a mechanical engineer. The interplay between software and hardware moves me the most. Eventually I started getting myself involved in developing software algorithms. Having had experiences ranging from designing suspension systems for a student formula racecar to developing object detection algorithms using Aritificial Intelligence for autonomous driving, I have constantly been on the pursuit of assimilating new concepts and learning new skills.
If not found at my desk working on a side projects like building robot vacuum cleaners, you can find me editing video contents, flying drones or enjoying a good ride under the sun.
