Face Shield vs COVID-19

A shield for the warriors at the cashier counters of supermarkets and other stores. Face shields are personal protective equipment devices that are used by many workers (e.g., medical, dental, veterinary) for protection of the facial area and associated mucous membranes (eyes, nose, mouth) from splashes, sprays, and spatter of body fluids. Check out this scientific paper which reviews face shields against infection control: Face shields for infection control: A review

How to make?

Packaging Cardboard boxes, Transparent sheets, Elastic bracelet threads, Cutting tools, and a stapler is all you need to make one in 10 minutes at home. If you have a 3D printer, you can also print more durable face shields. Visit this website for the .stl file. Big shoutout to "augment_3di" for bringing this design to my attention 👍

Over the night, I made a template which you can find in the file "FaceShield-DIY.pdf". The document also contains step-by-step instructions needed to build the face shield.
